The Computer Science Speedrun

SCHOOL

WEBSITE

Apr 11, 2023

BACKGROUND

During class one day, I was caught up and surpassed my classmates in all my computer science assignments. It wasn't the only time this happened, and usually, I helped my classmates when I had my work done. My teacher jokingly suggested I needed a good project to fulfill the empty time in the classroom. I was hesitant since I already had personal projects (such as this website), and there was no good reason to bridge away from the computer science assignments. The question still lingered: "How willing is this teacher to challenge me, and what is he offering for this project?".

THE MISSION

The mission was pushing my limits with my starting acquaintance with Python, though I knew I had to go even further for this project to be worth it. Because this project was worth three weeks of computer science work, and this had to be good. These were the minimum requirements:

  • Implantation of Python
  • Use of Python objects
  • Use of Python loops
  • Use of Python functions

Yes, this is easily doable, but I wanted my project to go above and beyond; I just had to think of the right idea.

WHAT'S THE HOLDUP?

I had no clear plan for this project due to its swift notice. I wanted to do something that impressed my teacher and met my expectations. My teacher suggested a Pygame-type project, but I only had one week, and it wasn't enough time. The night of day 5 came, and something hit my head like a ton of bricks: The due date. It was time to do something I hadn't done in computer science, run for the due date.

THE SPEEDRUN

With the few hours I had until the next school morning, I needed to complete this project to turn it in. Thankfully I came up with an outline to accomplish this mission, and as follows:

  • Goal: A website remake of a previous computer science CLI project
  • Python implantation: Since I'm using a website, Flask was perfect for this.
  • Website: I can use HTML, CSS, and JS to style the project to my expectations and save it from being a CLI-type project. With an interface instead of a command line, it can form into a unique and fun project.

With that, I got to work, as you can see from this image from the repository of my commits:

Image from: GitHub • Combo-Menu Repository Commits

Not only were things going according to plan every time I uploaded a stage of the project, but my confidence also soared tenfold. Each stage added or fixed something to the project, improving it that much more. As the night continued going from 10 pm to finally around 4 am, I completed Combo Menu Interface (CMI)!

THE AFTERMATH

My teacher loved my work after I presented it to him that evening in class. Even sending my parents and me a congratulatory email later for my work. Here is a quote from his email:

"And finally... just to repeat myself... outstanding work on the Combo Menu program. You not only met the objective, but you went above and beyond, even teaching yourself new things to expand your abilities. I'm impressed. That attitude and ethic will get you far in life! It's great to see this in a student, and is much appreciated."

My Teacher • Mar 01, 2023

From this project, it's essential to manage your time; because you may not be able to achieve what you desire. Don't be like me, and control your time to achieve greatness.
